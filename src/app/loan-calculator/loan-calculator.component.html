How to Use the Calculator

This Loan Calculator allows you to calculate the monthly payment and total payment based on the given loan amount, interest rate, loan term, term unit, and payment interval.

Enter the loan amount, interest rate, loan term, term unit (days, weeks, months, years), and payment interval (daily, weekly, monthly). Click the "Calculate Loan" button to perform the calculation. The monthly payment and total payment will be displayed below.
