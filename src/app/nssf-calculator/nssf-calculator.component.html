How to Use the Calculator

This NSSF Calculator allows you to calculate the NSSF contributions based on the given gross pay.

Enter the gross pay amount in the input field and click the "Calculate Contributions" button to perform the calculation. The employer and employee contributions, as well as the total contribution, will be displayed.
